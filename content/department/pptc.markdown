Passport Canada spent an estimated **\$290.4 in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 5 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 1 have at least one amendment (20%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 19% and the average number of amendments was 2. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$239.0K over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 4.3 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$548.6K. The longest contract was 7.25 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$1.5M.
