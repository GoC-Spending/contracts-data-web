Canadian Institutes of Health Research spent an estimated **\$4.8M in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 769 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 162 have at least one amendment (21%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 71% and the average number of amendments was 1.22. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$8.2M over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 0.66 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$61.0K. The longest contract was 6.77 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$2.0M.
