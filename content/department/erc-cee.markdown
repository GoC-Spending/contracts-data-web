RCMP External Review Committee spent an estimated **\$149.3K in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 12 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 2 have at least one amendment (17%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 39% and the average number of amendments was 1. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$17.5K over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 0.75 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$43.4K. The longest contract was 1.73 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$96.3K.
