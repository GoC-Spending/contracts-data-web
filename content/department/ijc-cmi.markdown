International Joint Commission spent an estimated **\$997.7K in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 174 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 3 have at least one amendment (2%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was -31% and the average number of amendments was 1. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$-11.8K over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 0.62 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$34.5K. The longest contract was 4 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$445.4K.
