Courts Administration Service spent an estimated **\$22.1M in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 1,196 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 145 have at least one amendment (12%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 98% and the average number of amendments was 1.34. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$11.4M over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 0.85 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$84.8K. The longest contract was 9.72 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$1.8M.
