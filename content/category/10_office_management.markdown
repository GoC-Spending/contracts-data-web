The Government of Canada spent an estimated **\$363.5M in 2021-2022** on office management contracts.

Across 26,961 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 2,355 have at least one amendment (9%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 71% and the average number of amendments was 1.55. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$387.7M over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 1.03 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$104.6K. The longest contract was 22.24 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$340.9M.
