Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council of Canada spent an estimated **\$1.3M in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 161 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 32 have at least one amendment (20%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 276% and the average number of amendments was 1.44. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$1.6M over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 1.05 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$54.1K. The longest contract was 8.27 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$484.1K.
