Status of Women Canada spent an estimated **\$12.3K in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 102 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 6 have at least one amendment (6%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 104% and the average number of amendments was 1. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$69.7K over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 0.6 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$30.8K. The longest contract was 4.98 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$170.5K.
