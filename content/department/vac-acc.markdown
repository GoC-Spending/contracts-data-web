Veterans Affairs Canada spent an estimated **\$199.5M in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 1,395 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 172 have at least one amendment (12%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 92% and the average number of amendments was 1.55. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$240.3M over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 0.76 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$1.1M. The longest contract was 10.87 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$560.3M.
