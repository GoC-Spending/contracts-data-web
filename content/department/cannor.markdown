Canadian Northern Economic Development Agency spent an estimated **\$382.9K in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 75 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 7 have at least one amendment (9%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 27% and the average number of amendments was 1.14. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$133.6K over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 0.74 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$38.1K. The longest contract was 5.12 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$238.2K.
