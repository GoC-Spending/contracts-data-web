Public Health Agency of Canada spent an estimated **\$2.1B in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 4,371 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 765 have at least one amendment (17%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 234% and the average number of amendments was 1.58. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$1.3B over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 0.98 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$1.6M. The longest contract was 17 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$602.1M.
