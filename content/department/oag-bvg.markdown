Office of the Auditor General of Canada spent an estimated **\$17.6M in 2021-2022** on contracts.

Across 1,182 contracts active since the start of the 2017-2018 fiscal year, 175 have at least one amendment (15%). Of contracts with amendments, the average increase in dollar value was 98% and the average number of amendments was 1.21. Cumulatively these amendments represent an increase of \$13.5M over the original contract values.

The average contract duration was 1.07 years, and the average contract value (not including contracts under \$10k) was \$61.5K. The longest contract was 10.76 years. The largest contract by value (including amendments) was \$3.7M.
